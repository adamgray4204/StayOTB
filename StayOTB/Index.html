Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

We do not keep your data. Stay OTB is designed with privacy as a core principle.

Data Collection and Storage

Stay OTB stores all your ride data, technical feature tracking, and progression history locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal riding data on our servers.

What We Don't Collect

  • GPS coordinates or location data
  • Ride statistics or performance data
  • Personal information beyond what you voluntarily provide
  • Device identifiers or usage analytics

Optional Data Sharing

The only time your data may be shared is when you explicitly choose to export or share specific rides using the app's built-in sharing features. This sharing is always initiated by you and under your complete control.

Contact Form

When you submit the contact form on this support page, we only use your email address to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to mailing lists or share your contact information with third parties.

App Store Data

The app may collect standard iOS analytics through the App Store as permitted by your device settings. This data is managed by Apple according to their privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We will update this privacy policy if our practices change. Any updates will be posted on this page.
